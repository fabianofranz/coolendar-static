coolendar is not only calendar or task app: it's a whole new planning experience

We believe it is time for a new level of planning software. Something simple that works for you, without the noise of handling a calendar or tasks app.

why is this different?

Instead of these overhead matrix-like calendar views, now you have a simple list of your planning entries. That's all you need, you will know why.
Use custom #tags to get organized. Create group plans in a social way together with your @buddies.
Efficient, error-proof and multiple alerts to find you wherever you are (e-mail, messenger, web, desktop, mobile, twitter).
No more recurrence in your life: when you get notified about a planned event, you decide if you need to reschedule it or not. With one click.
Forget durations: it's a poison concept and doesn't allow you to be concise and consistent. You just don't need it.
Smooth: just tell your coolendar when (date and time) and what, in human language. It will be understood, don't worry. Example:
tomorrow 8am make sure to grab the #umbrella!
